Privacy Policy

Funhaus Wilderkind Print Co — Staging App

Effective Date: 22 April 2026  |  Last Updated: 22 April 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Funhaus Wilderkind Print Co Staging App ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application. The App is a private internal tool developed by Funhaus Wilderkind Print Co for the purpose of automating the scheduling and publication of Pinterest content related to our Etsy storefront.

By authorising or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Who We Are

  • Operator: Funhaus Wilderkind Print Co
  • Contact Email: jamesblanckenberg@gmail.com
  • Application Type: Private, single-user automation tool
  • Intended Users: The App owner only (operating in Pinterest Trial Access mode)

The App collects and processes the following information solely from the authorised Pinterest account of the App owner:

4. How We Use Information

Information collected by the App is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with the Pinterest API on behalf of the App owner
  • Create, schedule, and publish pins to the App owner's Pinterest boards
  • Maintain a local queue and audit log of pin activity
  • Detect and recover from API errors, rate limits, and token expiry
  • Prevent duplicate pin creation

We do not use any data for advertising, profiling, analytics resale, machine learning training, or any purpose unrelated to the operation of the App.

5. How We Store and Protect Information

  • All credentials and tokens are stored locally on the App owner's personal computer with operating-system-level access controls (Windows BitLocker / NTFS permissions).
  • No data is transmitted to any third-party server other than Pinterest's official API endpoints (api.pinterest.com).
  • No data is uploaded to any cloud service operated by Funhaus Wilderkind Print Co.
  • Local SQLite databases and log files are restricted to the App owner's user account.
  • OAuth tokens are never logged in plaintext, committed to source control, or shared.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, lease, or disclose any information collected by the App to any third party. The App operates entirely between the App owner's local machine and the Pinterest API.

The only data transmission performed by the App is:

  • Outbound: Authenticated requests to https://api.pinterest.com/v5/*
  • Inbound: Responses from the Pinterest API

7. Data Retention

  • OAuth tokens: Retained until the App owner revokes access via Pinterest settings or manually deletes the local credentials file.
  • Pin queue and logs: Retained locally for audit purposes; manually purged at the App owner's discretion.
  • Pinterest user data: Not retained beyond what is required to operate the App.

The App owner may delete all stored data at any time by removing the local application directory.

8. User Rights

As the App is a private single-user tool operated by the App owner, the App owner has full control over all data the App processes, including:

  • The right to access all stored data (all data is local and human-readable)
  • The right to delete all stored data at any time
  • The right to revoke Pinterest API access at any time via Pinterest account settings

9. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the following third-party service:

  • Pinterest API v5 — governed by the Pinterest Privacy Policy and Pinterest Developer Guidelines

The App does not integrate with any other third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising service.

10. Children's Privacy
